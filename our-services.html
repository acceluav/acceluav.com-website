AGRICULTURE SOLUTIONS

  • We offer a pesticide spraying service for all types of crops using agriculture drones.
  • In the agriculture industry technology is quite sought after and usage of UAVs in farming has totally revolutionized the precision agriculture scene globally.
  • We use precision spray UAVs which can help in specifically targeting liquid fertilizers and pesticides which would significantly reduce the wastage and is 40-60 times faster than the traditional spraying methods.
  • UAVs ensure safe operations as there is no direct exposure to the chemicals unlike the conventional methods of spraying.
